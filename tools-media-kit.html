How it works

From profile to brand-ready link, in four steps.

Set up your media kit once, share the link forever. Update your stats anytime — the link stays the same.

Step 1
Add your stats
Follower count and profile URL per platform (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, etc.) and your engagement rate. Manual entry — you control what's shown.
Step 2
Pick your theme
Choose from preset color themes or set custom colors. Match your brand, not a generic template.
Step 3
Set your rate card
What does a Reel cost? A Story? A bundle? Make it crystal clear so brands don't have to ask.
Step 4
Publish & share
Toggle publish and your media kit goes live at ryxa.io/mediakit/yourname. Send it to brands, paste in pitches, link in your bio.
Features

A serious media kit, no design skills needed.

Skip the Canva templates and the Google Doc patchwork. A live media kit page that works on every device, with the info brands actually look for.

Per-platform followers
Enter your follower count and profile URL for each platform you're on — Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X, and others. Plus your engagement rate.
Clear rate card
Reels, Stories, posts, packages. Set your rates upfront so brands self-qualify before they reach out.
Custom theme MAX
Creator Max members can set custom colors and a custom background image to match their personal branding instead of using the preset themes.
Built-in engagement rate guide
Includes the industry-standard formula and benchmarks (1-3% typical, 6%+ exceptional) so you know what to enter and what's good.
Shareable link
ryxa.io/mediakit/yourname. Clean, professional, no PDF attachments to wrestle with.
Publish toggle
Flip your media kit on or off anytime. Unpublished kits return a 404 — bring it back live whenever you're ready to pitch.

FAQ

Common questions about Media Kit.

Why not just send brands a Google Doc or PDF?
+
You can. But a live link looks more professional and updates instantly when your stats change. Brands take you more seriously when your media kit looks like a real product, not a Frankenstein doc you slapped together.
Do I need to manually update my stats?
+
Yes. You enter your stats and update them when they change.
Can I have multiple rate cards for different services?
+
Yes. Add as many custom rate items as you want — single Reel, package deals, story bundles, monthly retainers, etc. Each with its own label, price, and notes.
Is the URL always public?
+
It's unlisted but accessible to anyone with the link. You can also unpublish it anytime if you don't want it accessible at all.
What's included in Pro?
+
Media Kit, Link in Bio, page view analytics, and Pro themes. Pro is $10/mo. Creator Max ($20/mo) adds Brand Deal CRM, Script Builder, custom themes, and removes the daily caps on QR codes, image conversions, and invoices.
