FAQ

Questions about Subscribers

Is it really unlimited and free?
+
Yes. Collecting and storing subscribers is free for everyone, with no cap and no per-subscriber fees. Build a list of any size without it costing you more.
Where do subscribers come from?
+
From email opt-ins on your link in bio, from people who buy your courses, booking sessions, and digital products, and from your own CSV imports or manual additions. Each subscriber is tagged with the source they joined under.
Can I export my list?
+
Anytime. Download your full list as a CSV with each subscriber's email, source, opt-in status, and join date. It is your audience, so there is no lock-in.
Does Subscribers send emails or newsletters?
+
Subscribers is for building, organizing, and exporting your list. It is not a newsletter sender. Export your opt-in list and send through your preferred email platform.
Do my subscribers know they joined a list?
+
Yes. Opt-ins are tracked per subscriber, so you can see who agreed to hear from you and filter to just those people before exporting. You can also remove anyone from your list at any time.
