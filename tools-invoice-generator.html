How it works

From details to a polished PDF, in four steps.

Fill in the basics, list the work, and download. No accounting software required.

Step 1
Add your details
Your name or business, contact info, and logo if you have one.
Step 2
Add the client
Brand or client name, contact email, and address.
Step 3
List the work
Line items with description, quantity, rate. Add taxes if needed. Total calculates automatically.
Step 4
Download as PDF
Clean, professional layout. Send to the client by email or upload to your accounting software.

FAQ

Common questions about Invoice Generator.

Is the invoice generator really free?
+
Yes, with a daily limit of 3 invoices on the Free tier. Pro removes the cap. Includes the clean template, line items, tax, and PDF export.
Does this integrate with the Brand Deal CRM?
+
Yes — if you're on Creator Max. You can attach invoices directly to deals in the CRM and email them to brands through the deal portal. Standalone use is free for everyone.
Are my invoices saved?
+
The standalone Invoice Generator doesn't store invoices — you fill in the form and download the PDF. If you upload an invoice into the Brand Deal CRM (Creator Max), that PDF is attached to the deal record and accessible from the dashboard.
What about VAT / sales tax?
+
Set a tax percentage on the totals row and the math handles the rest. We don't auto-calculate region-specific rates — enter whatever rate applies to you.
Can I send the invoice directly from Ryxa?
+
From the Brand Deal CRM (Creator Max), yes — emails the invoice to the brand contact. Standalone, you download the PDF and send via your normal email.
