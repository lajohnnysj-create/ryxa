FAQ

Common questions about Invoicing.

Is invoicing really free?
+
Yes, and it's unlimited. Create, send, and get paid on as many invoices as you need on any plan. Ryxa takes 0% of your invoice payments, only Stripe's standard card-processing fee applies when a client pays by card. Pro adds your logo for branded invoices.
How do clients pay me?
+
Connect your Stripe account and clients can pay the invoice by card directly on its public page. Payment goes straight to your Stripe account. Prefer another method? You can instead show your Zelle, Venmo, or other payment details on the invoice.
Are my invoices saved?
+
Yes. Every invoice is saved to your account so you can manage, resend, and track it. Your invoice list shows draft, pending, and paid at a glance, and paid invoices flow into your revenue analytics.
Do invoices get marked paid automatically?
+
When a client pays by card through Stripe, the invoice is marked paid automatically and locked. If you're paid another way (Zelle, Venmo, bank transfer), you can mark it paid yourself.
What about VAT / sales tax?
+
Set a tax percentage on the totals row and the math handles the rest. We don't auto-calculate region-specific rates, enter whatever rate applies to you.
