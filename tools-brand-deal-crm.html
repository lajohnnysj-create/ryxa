How it works

From inquiry to invoice paid, in four steps.

Built around the actual workflow of a creator running brand partnerships, not generic project management software bent into shape.

Step 1
Add the deal
Title, brand, amount, dates, deliverables. Save as a draft while you negotiate.
Step 2

Step 3

FAQ

Common questions about Brand Deal CRM.

Does the brand need a Ryxa account to use the portal?
+
No. The brand portal is completely standalone. They get a private link plus a 6-digit PIN, and they can view deal details, download files, and message you, all without signing up. The portal is locked to that one deal, so they can't see anything else.
How are contracts and invoices stored?
+
Securely in our cloud storage with strict access controls. Only you (the creator) and the brand contact you've shared the portal with can access the files. PDFs up to 10MB. Files persist as long as your account is active and are removed when you delete the deal.
Can I revoke the brand's portal access later?
+
Yes. One click revokes the link and PIN, locking the brand out immediately. You can also generate a fresh link anytime. Cancelled deals automatically have their share access revoked.
Does this connect to my bank or payment processor?
+
Not yet. Right now you mark deals as paid manually after the brand sends payment. We send invoices to the brand by email, but the actual payment happens through your usual channels (bank transfer, Stripe, PayPal, etc.).
Is there a limit on how many deals I can track?
+
No. Track as many brand deals as you want, with full history preserved. Your dashboard shows total revenue across any date range you pick (last 7, 14, 30 days, or custom).
