How to calculate engagement rate
The industry-standard formula:
(Avg. likes + comments per post) ÷ Followers × 100
Quick method: Take your last 10 posts. Add up all likes + comments. Divide by 10 (your average). Divide that by your follower count. Multiply by 100.
What's a good rate?
• 1-3%. Typical for accounts with 100K+ followers
• 3-6%. Strong engagement
• 6%+. Exceptional engagement
Report your primary platform's rate (usually Instagram). Brands care most about this number.
