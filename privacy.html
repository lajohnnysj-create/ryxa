Ryxa™ is a product of MRLA Media LLC, operating at ryxa.io. We provide creator tools including Link in Bio, Media Kit, Course Builder, 1:1 Booking, Digital Products, Brand Deal CRM, Analytics, Grid Planner, Follow-Back Audit, Script Builder, Design Studio, AI Thumbnail Analyzer, AI Contract Analyzer, Photo Editor, Invoice Generator, PDF Signer, and QR Generator.

This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what rights you have. We try to be honest and straightforward, no dense legalese.

2. Data we collect

Account information: Your email address and a hashed password, handled by Supabase Auth. We never see your plaintext password.

Profile: Your chosen username, used to form your public URLs (ryxa.io/username).

Billing: Your subscription status (Free, Pro, Creator Max), tier, and Stripe customer ID. All payment information (card numbers, billing address) is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

Content you create:

  • Link in Bio: Display name, bio, avatar photo, links, hero images, theme, social handles
  • Media Kit: Display name, headshot, audience stats, rate card, past collaborations, contact details
  • Script Builder (Pro): Script titles, hooks, content blocks, section headers
  • Brand Deal CRM (Max only): Deal details, brand names, brand contact names and emails, deal amounts and dates, deliverables, uploaded contracts (PDFs), uploaded invoices (PDFs), and messages exchanged with brands through the secure portal
  • Course Builder (Max only): Course titles, descriptions, cover images, modules, lessons (text, uploaded video and audio files, downloadable lesson files), pricing, and enrollment data
  • 1:1 Booking (Max only): Service titles, descriptions, cover images, pricing, session duration, booking type preference, and scheduling links
  • Digital Products (Max only): Product titles, descriptions, cover images, pricing, uploaded product files (PDFs, images, video, audio, archives, and other supported formats up to the size limit), buyer purchase records, and download access metadata
  • Ryxa Hub (Buyers): Course enrollment records, lesson progress, 1:1 Booking records, and digital product purchase and download records
  • Analytics (Max only): Page view counts and revenue tracking data for your published pages
  • Grid Planner (Pro only): Photos you upload for grid planning
  • Follower Audit: Lists you mark as "whitelist" or "snake list", audit history (Pro)
  • Invoice Generator: Uploaded logo (Pro), invoice details are generated client-side and not stored
  • Chatbox (Pro/Max): Your AI chat conversations and messages, stored so you can return to them. You can delete any conversation at any time.

Usage limits: For AI-powered features (Chatbox, Script Builder, Bio Writer, Thumbnail Analyzer, Contract Analyzer, and similar), we record each call you make in our database so we can enforce per-tier rate limits and prevent abuse. We log your user ID, which endpoint was called, and the timestamp. We do not log the prompts, inputs, or AI responses themselves in this rate-limit record. Records older than what we need for enforcement are periodically deleted.

3. What we do NOT collect

PDF Signer is fully client-side. PDF files you sign are processed entirely in your browser. They never touch our servers. Once you close the tab, the file is gone.

AI-powered features (Chatbox, Design Studio, Script Builder, Bio Writer, Caption Generator, Thumbnail Analyzer, Contract Analyzer, Alt-text Generator) send content to our AI provider (Anthropic) for processing. For one-shot tools (everything except Chatbox), text and images are sent only during the request and are not stored by us or by Anthropic after the response is returned. Contract PDFs are extracted client-side, only the text is sent, never the original file. For Chatbox specifically, your messages and the AI's responses are stored on our servers so you can return to past conversations; you can delete any conversation at any time from the Chatbox sidebar. We don't use your AI inputs or outputs to train AI models.

Photo Editor is fully client-side. Images you edit are processed in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.

Follower Audit processes locally. When you upload Instagram's export files, the data is parsed in your browser. The raw files never leave your device.

We do not collect Instagram passwords, login credentials, or any data from your Instagram account beyond what you explicitly upload via official Instagram data exports.

Ryxa lets you connect your Instagram Business or Creator account to keep your audience data synced with your Ryxa profile and media kit. Connecting is always optional, Ryxa works fully without it.

How the connection works. When you click "Connect Instagram," you're sent to Meta's official OAuth authorization page. You log in to Instagram directly with Meta, Ryxa never sees your password. After you approve, Meta sends Ryxa a long-lived access token (valid for approximately 60 days, refreshable) that lets us call the Instagram Graph API on your behalf.   • Profile information: Instagram username, profile picture, account type, and display name
  • Account totals: follower count, following count, and media count
  • 30-day account insights: reach, profile views, total interactions, and views (formerly impressions)
  • Recent media metadata: up to 25 most recent posts, including like and comment counts, post type, and timestamps
  • Per-reel view counts and per-story view counts (where available, used for engagement averages)
  • Audience demographics for accounts with 100+ followers: top countries, top cities, age ranges, and gender breakdown

How we use this data. Instagram data powers features you have activated in Ryxa, such as:

  • Auto-filling your Ryxa media kit with current follower counts, engagement metrics, and audience demographics that brands can view on your public media kit page
  • Calculating engagement rates and per-post averages for display on your media kit and dashboard
  • Showing your follower demographics (gender, age, top countries, top cities) on your public media kit so brands can evaluate audience fit

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your Instagram data with third parties. We do NOT use it for advertising. We do NOT use it to train AI models.

How we store this data. Your Instagram access token and the data we receive from Meta are stored in our Supabase database, encrypted at rest. Access is restricted by row-level security policies so only you and our authenticated systems can read your data. We refresh the cached data automatically every 24 hours so your media kit stays current, and you can also trigger a manual refresh from your dashboard at any time.

How to disconnect or delete your Instagram data. You can disconnect Instagram from Ryxa at any time:

  • Inside Ryxa: Go to Settings → Connected Accounts → Disconnect Instagram. This revokes our access token on Meta's side and immediately deletes all cached Instagram data from our database.

Compliance with Meta Platform Terms. Ryxa's use of information received from the Instagram Graph API and Meta APIs adheres to the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We will not transfer Meta-derived data to any ad network, data broker, or other advertising or monetization-related service.

14. Where your data is stored

Your primary account data (database records, authentication, cover images) is stored in the United States in AWS us-east-2 (Ohio), via Supabase.

Creator-uploaded files for Digital Products and Course Lessons (PDFs, images, audio, archives, and other supported formats) are stored on Cloudflare R2, a globally distributed object storage network. Course lesson videos are stored and delivered through Bunny.net's Bunny Stream service, a globally distributed video CDN. Cloudflare and Bunny may route or replicate this data across multiple regions to ensure availability and delivery performance. Files in R2 are stored in private buckets and only delivered to authorized recipients via short-lived signed URLs.
