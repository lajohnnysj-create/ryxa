Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

1. Who we are

Ryxa is a product of MRLA Media LLC, operating at ryxa.io. We provide creator tools including Link in Bio, Media Kit, Grid Planner, Follow-Back Audit, Script Builder, Invoice Generator, Tax Tracker, PDF Signer, QR Generator, and Image Converter.

This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what rights you have. We try to be honest and straightforward — no dense legalese.

2. Data we collect

Account information: Your email address and a hashed password, handled by Supabase Auth. We never see your plaintext password.

Profile: Your chosen username, used to form your public URLs (ryxa.io/username).

Billing: Your subscription status (Free, Pro, Creator Max), tier, and Stripe customer ID. All payment information (card numbers, billing address) is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

Content you create:

  • Link in Bio: Display name, bio, avatar photo, links, hero images, theme, social handles
  • Media Kit: Display name, headshot, audience stats, rate card, past collaborations, contact details
  • Script Builder (Max only): Script titles, hooks, content blocks, section headers
  • Tax Tracker (Pro only): Income entries you log, including amounts, sources, and dates
  • Grid Planner (Pro only): Photos you upload for grid planning
  • Follower Audit: Lists you mark as "whitelist" or "snake list", audit history (Pro)
  • Invoice Generator: Uploaded logo (Pro), invoice details are generated client-side and not stored

Usage limits: For free-tier tools, daily/monthly usage counts are stored in your browser's local storage, not on our servers.

3. What we do NOT collect

PDF Signer is fully client-side. PDF files you sign are processed entirely in your browser. They never touch our servers. Once you close the tab, the file is gone.

Image Converter is fully client-side. Images you convert are processed in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.

Follower Audit processes locally. When you upload Instagram's export files, the data is parsed in your browser. The raw files never leave your device.

We do not collect Instagram passwords, login credentials, or any data from your Instagram account beyond what you explicitly upload via official Instagram data exports.

4. What's public vs private

Public — intended to be publicly visible when you publish:

  • Your Link in Bio page (if published): username, bio text, avatar, links, photos, theme
  • Your Media Kit page (if published): display name, headshot, bio, rate card, stats, collaborations
  • When someone shares your public URL, the page's avatar/headshot appears as a preview image in messaging apps

Private — only visible to you:

  • Scripts, tax entries, grid plans, whitelist/snake list, audit history, unpublished drafts
  • Email address, subscription details, uploaded invoice logos

5. How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in
  • Process payments via Stripe and manage your subscription
  • Store and display your content on your dashboard
  • Render your published pages (Link in Bio, Media Kit) to visitors
  • Send important account emails (password resets, billing receipts, service notices)
  • Diagnose and fix bugs when they occur

We do not: sell your data, share it with advertisers, use it to train AI models, or use it for any purpose unrelated to operating this service.

6. Cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Authentication cookies (Supabase) — keep you signed in
  • Session cookies — remember your preferences during a visit
  • Local storage — track daily/monthly tool usage limits for free users, and cache preferences
  • Privacy-respecting analytics — we may use a minimal analytics tool to understand aggregate usage patterns (which tools are popular, how users find us). This never identifies you personally or tracks you across other sites.

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, Facebook Pixel, or invasive third-party trackers. If we ever add a new category of cookie, we'll update this policy and refresh the consent banner.

7. Subprocessors

We use these trusted third-party services to operate Ryxa:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, file storage. Primary data stored in AWS us-east-2 (Ohio, USA). Privacy policy
  • Vercel — web hosting and serverless functions. USA-based. Privacy policy
  • Stripe — payment processing. PCI-DSS compliant. Privacy policy
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (password resets, receipts). Privacy policy

8. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored primarily in the United States (AWS us-east-2, Ohio). If you are accessing the service from outside the US, by using Ryxa you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in the United States.

9. Data retention

Active accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active.

Cancelled Pro/Max subscriptions: Pro or Max feature data (tax entries, grid plans, scripts, custom themes, media kit, audit history) is retained for 30 days after downgrade in case you resubscribe. After 30 days, this data is cleared.

Account deletion: If you request account deletion, all your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

10. Your rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

  • Access your data — request a copy of what we store
  • Correct inaccurate data — most fields can be edited directly in your dashboard
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a common format
  • Object to how your data is processed

California residents (CCPA): You have additional rights to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion, and to opt out of any "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

EU/UK residents (GDPR): We process your data under the legal bases of contract (providing the service you signed up for) and legitimate interest (operating and improving the service).

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@ryxa.io. We'll respond within 30 days.

11. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS everywhere), industry-standard authentication practices, row-level security policies on all database tables, and limited access to production data. No online service is 100% secure, but we work to minimize risk. If we ever experience a data breach that affects you, we will notify you promptly as required by law.

12. Children's privacy

Ryxa is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has signed up, please contact us at hello@ryxa.io and we will delete the account.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top. For material changes, we will notify you by email or via an in-app notice. Continued use of the service after updates constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
