Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

FindTheSnakes ("we", "us", "our") operates findthesnakes.com and provides a suite of creator tools including a Follower Audit, Image Converter, QR Code Generator, and Invoice Generator. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

Account data: When you create an account, we collect your email address and store it securely via Supabase.

Subscription data: If you upgrade to Pro, we store your subscription status and tier. Payment information is handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your credit card details.

Tool data: For Pro users, we store feature data you create — such as your whitelist, snake list, audit history, unfollower log, and uploaded invoice logo. This data is associated with your account and stored securely.

Usage limits: Daily and monthly usage limits for tools (Image Converter, QR Generator, Invoice Generator) are tracked in your browser's local storage — not on our servers.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not store the raw files you use with our tools. The Follower Audit processes your Instagram export files in your browser — the file contents are not stored on our servers. The Image Converter converts images in your browser — your images are not stored on our servers. Your invoice logo is stored in Supabase if you choose to upload it as a Pro user.

We do not collect your Instagram password, login credentials, or any information from your Instagram account. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the FindTheSnakes service. This includes authenticating your account, managing your subscription, storing your Pro feature data, and sending important account-related emails (such as password resets and billing receipts). We do not use your data for advertising and we never sell your data to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

Supabase: We use Supabase for authentication and database storage. Your account data is stored on Supabase servers.

Stripe: We use Stripe to process payments. Stripe handles all payment data securely and in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.

Vercel: Our website is hosted on Vercel. Standard server access logs may be collected by Vercel as part of hosting.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your Pro subscription, your Pro-specific data (audit history, snake list, whitelist, unfollower log) is retained for 30 days before being cleared. Your account remains active on the Free plan. If you request account deletion, we will delete all your data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To request data deletion or a copy of your data, email us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

FindTheSnakes is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
