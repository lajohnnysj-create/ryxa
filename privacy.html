3. What we do NOT collect

PDF Signer is fully client-side. PDF files you sign are processed entirely in your browser. They never touch our servers. Once you close the tab, the file is gone.

AI-powered features (Chatbox, Design Studio, Script Builder, Bio Writer, Caption Generator, Thumbnail Analyzer, Contract Analyzer, Alt-text Generator) send content to our AI provider (Anthropic) for processing. For one-shot tools (everything except Chatbox), text and images are sent only during the request and are not stored by us or by Anthropic after the response is returned. Contract PDFs are extracted client-side — only the text is sent, never the original file. For Chatbox specifically, your messages and the AI's responses are stored on our servers so you can return to past conversations; you can delete any conversation at any time from the Chatbox sidebar. We don't use your AI inputs or outputs to train AI models.

Photo Editor is fully client-side. Images you edit are processed in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.

Follower Audit processes locally. When you upload Instagram's export files, the data is parsed in your browser. The raw files never leave your device.

We do not collect Instagram passwords, login credentials, or any data from your Instagram account beyond what you explicitly upload via official Instagram data exports.

8. Connecting your Instagram account

Ryxa lets you connect your Instagram Business or Creator account to keep your audience data synced with your Ryxa profile and media kit. Connecting is always optional — Ryxa works fully without it.

How the connection works. When you click "Connect Instagram," you're sent to Meta's official OAuth authorization page. You log in to Instagram directly with Meta — Ryxa never sees your password. After you approve, Meta sends Ryxa a long-lived access token (valid for approximately 60 days, refreshable) that lets us call the Instagram Graph API on your behalf. You can revoke this access at any time (see below).

What we receive from Meta. When you grant the permissions instagram_business_basic and instagram_business_manage_insights, Ryxa may receive:

  • Profile information: Instagram username, profile picture, account type, and display name
  • Account totals: follower count, following count, and media count
  • 30-day account insights: reach, profile views, total interactions, and views (formerly impressions)
  • Recent media metadata: up to 25 most recent posts, including like and comment counts, post type, and timestamps
  • Per-reel view counts and per-story view counts (where available, used for engagement averages)
  • Audience demographics for accounts with 100+ followers: top countries, top cities, age ranges, and gender breakdown

How we use this data. Instagram data powers features you have activated in Ryxa, such as:

  • Auto-filling your Ryxa media kit with current follower counts, engagement metrics, and audience demographics that brands can view on your public media kit page
  • Calculating engagement rates and per-post averages for display on your media kit and dashboard
  • Showing your follower demographics (gender, age, top countries, top cities) on your public media kit so brands can evaluate audience fit

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your Instagram data with third parties. We do NOT use it for advertising. We do NOT use it to train AI models.

How we store this data. Your Instagram access token and the data we receive from Meta are stored in our Supabase database, encrypted at rest. Access is restricted by row-level security policies so only you and our authenticated systems can read your data. We refresh the cached data automatically every 24 hours so your media kit stays current, and you can also trigger a manual refresh from your dashboard at any time.

How to disconnect or delete your Instagram data. You can disconnect Instagram from Ryxa at any time:

  • Inside Ryxa: Go to Settings → Connected Accounts → Disconnect Instagram. This revokes our access token on Meta's side and immediately deletes all cached Instagram data from our database.
  • Inside Instagram: Go to Instagram Settings → Apps and Websites → Active → Find "Ryxa" → Remove. This revokes our access on Meta's side immediately. Meta will also send our endpoint a deletion notification, and we will delete your data within 24 hours.
  • By request: Email hello@ryxa.io and we will permanently delete all Instagram data associated with your account within 30 days.

Compliance with Meta Platform Terms. Ryxa's use of information received from the Instagram Graph API and Meta APIs adheres to the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies. We will not transfer Meta-derived data to any ad network, data broker, or other advertising or monetization-related service.

Note on affiliation. Ryxa is an independent platform. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Instagram or Meta Platforms, Inc.
