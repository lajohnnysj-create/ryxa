Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026 · Last updated: April 22, 2026

1. Who we are

Ryxa is a product of MRLA Media LLC, operating at ryxa.io. We provide creator tools including Link in Bio, Media Kit, Brand Deal CRM, Analytics, Grid Planner, Follow-Back Audit, AI Script Builder, AI Design Studio, AI Thumbnail Analyzer, AI Contract Analyzer, Photo Editor, Invoice Generator, PDF Signer, and QR Generator.

This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what rights you have. We try to be honest and straightforward — no dense legalese.

2. Data we collect

Account information: Your email address and a hashed password, handled by Supabase Auth. We never see your plaintext password.

Profile: Your chosen username, used to form your public URLs (ryxa.io/username).

Billing: Your subscription status (Free, Pro, Creator Max), tier, and Stripe customer ID. All payment information (card numbers, billing address) is handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

Content you create:

  • Link in Bio: Display name, bio, avatar photo, links, hero images, theme, social handles
  • Media Kit: Display name, headshot, audience stats, rate card, past collaborations, contact details
  • Script Builder (Pro): Script titles, hooks, content blocks, section headers
  • Brand Deal CRM (Max only): Deal details, brand names, brand contact names and emails, deal amounts and dates, deliverables, uploaded contracts (PDFs), uploaded invoices (PDFs), and messages exchanged with brands through the secure portal
  • Course Builder (Max only): Course titles, descriptions, cover images, modules, lessons (text and video embed URLs), pricing, and enrollment data
  • 1:1 Private Coaching (Max only): Service titles, descriptions, cover images, pricing, session duration, booking type preference, and scheduling links
  • Learning Hub (Buyers): Course enrollment records, lesson progress, and coaching booking records
  • Analytics (Max only): Page view counts and revenue tracking data for your published pages
  • Grid Planner (Pro only): Photos you upload for grid planning
  • Follower Audit: Lists you mark as "whitelist" or "snake list", audit history (Pro)
  • Invoice Generator: Uploaded logo (Pro), invoice details are generated client-side and not stored

Usage limits: For free-tier tools, daily/monthly usage counts are stored in your browser's local storage, not on our servers.

3. What we do NOT collect

PDF Signer is fully client-side. PDF files you sign are processed entirely in your browser. They never touch our servers. Once you close the tab, the file is gone.

AI-powered features (Design Studio, Script Builder, Thumbnail Analyzer, Contract Analyzer) send content to our AI provider (Anthropic) for processing. Text and images are sent only during the request and are not stored by us or our AI provider after processing. Contract PDFs are extracted client-side — only the text is sent, never the original file.

Photo Editor is fully client-side. Images you edit are processed in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.

Follower Audit processes locally. When you upload Instagram's export files, the data is parsed in your browser. The raw files never leave your device.

We do not collect Instagram passwords, login credentials, or any data from your Instagram account beyond what you explicitly upload via official Instagram data exports.

4. What's public vs private

Public — intended to be publicly visible when you publish:

  • Your Link in Bio page (if published): username, bio text, avatar, links, photos, theme
  • Your Media Kit page (if published): display name, headshot, bio, rate card, stats, collaborations
  • Your Course landing pages (if published): course title, description, cover image, curriculum, price, and your username
  • Your Coaching landing pages (if published): service title, description, cover image, price, duration, and your username
  • When someone shares your public URL, the page's avatar/headshot appears as a preview image in messaging apps

Private — only visible to you:

  • Scripts, grid plans, whitelist/snake list, audit history, unpublished drafts
  • Brand Deal CRM data: deals, brand contact info, contracts, invoices, and messages (only visible to you and to brand contacts you explicitly share a deal portal with)
  • Course enrollment data, student progress, and revenue records
  • Coaching booking records and buyer contact information
  • Email address, subscription details, uploaded invoice logos

5. How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate your account and keep you signed in
  • Process payments via Stripe and manage your subscription
  • Store and display your content on your dashboard
  • Render your published pages (Link in Bio, Media Kit, Course landing pages, Coaching landing pages) to visitors
  • Send important account emails (password resets, billing receipts, service notices)
  • Send transactional emails on your behalf when you use the Brand Deal CRM — for example, notifying a brand contact you've added that you've sent them a message or invoice through the secure portal
  • Send purchase confirmation and booking notification emails to buyers of your courses and coaching sessions, and to you as the creator when a purchase or booking is made
  • Process payments via Stripe Connect for course and coaching purchases, routing payments directly to the creator's connected Stripe account
  • Diagnose and fix bugs when they occur

We do not: sell your data, share it with advertisers, use it to train AI models, or use it for any purpose unrelated to operating this service.

6. Cookies

  • Authentication cookies (Supabase) — keep you signed in

  • Local storage — track daily/monthly tool usage limits for free users, and cache preferences

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, Facebook Pixel, or invasive third-party trackers. If we ever add a new category of cookie, we'll update this policy and refresh the consent banner.

9. Data retention

Account deletion: You can request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing hello@ryxa.io. We'll delete everything within 30 days of receiving your request.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@ryxa.io. We'll respond within 30 days.
