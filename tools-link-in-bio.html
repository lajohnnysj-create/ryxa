FAQ

Common questions about Link in Bio.

Is it really free?
+
Yes. The Free plan gets you a custom URL, profile photo, bio, social icons, 50 links, 1 featured link, and all 12 preset themes. Pro ($10/mo) unlocks 200 links, 3 featured, full theme customization with custom backgrounds and fonts, hero avatar display, and removes Ryxa branding from your page. Creator Max ($20/mo) extends that to 1,000 links, 10 featured, hero links, and lets you sell courses, coaching, and digital products directly from your bio.
Can I use my own domain?
+
Not yet, but you can still use your own domain. Most domain registrars (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google Domains, Squarespace) let you set up domain forwarding for free, which sends visitors of yourdomain.com to ryxa.io/yourname automatically. Native custom domain support is on our roadmap for a future Pro/Max feature.
How is this different from Linktree or Beacons?
+
Honestly, the base feature set is similar. The difference is Ryxa bundles 9 other creator tools alongside it (Brand Deal CRM, Script Builder, Media Kit, etc.) at the same price point Linktree charges just for their Pro tier.
Can I see how many people visit my bio?
+
Yes. Your dashboard shows total page views over any date range you pick — last 7, 14, 30 days, or custom. Per-link click tracking is on the roadmap.
What if I want to take my page down?
+
Toggle 'unpublished' in the dashboard and your page returns a 404 immediately. Toggle back to publish whenever you want. Your data stays — we don't delete anything.
