FAQ

Common questions about Link in Bio.

Is Link in Bio actually free?
+
Yes. Building and publishing your link-in-bio page is free on Ryxa. The Free plan gets you your page, your links, sections, and drag-to-reorder. Custom branding (your own colors, fonts, and background) is on Pro. Selling courses, digital products, and 1:1 sessions through your page is on Max.
Can I sell products through my link in bio?
+
Yes, on the Max plan. Once you upgrade, you can add courses, digital products, and 1:1 booking pages to your bio as links. When a follower taps one, they land on that product's checkout page. Payments go directly to your Stripe account, and Ryxa takes 0% transaction fees on top of standard Stripe processing.
Does Ryxa take a cut of my sales?
+
No. Ryxa takes 0% transaction fees on every sale through your link-in-bio page. The only fees are Stripe's standard processing fees (around 2.9% + 30 cents per transaction), which go to Stripe, not to Ryxa.
What's my page URL?
+
Your page lives at ryxa.io/yourhandle. You pick the handle when you sign up. Use the same handle you use on Instagram or TikTok if it's available, so your audience finds you easily.
How is this different from Linktree or Beacons?
+
Linktree and Beacons are mostly link lists. Ryxa is a creator business in a single link. You can list your socials and content like you would on Linktree, but you can also actually sell your courses, digital products, and 1:1 sessions on the same page, with hosting, checkout, and delivery handled for you. One link, one business, no extra tools.
