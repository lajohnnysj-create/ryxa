The short answer

Markiplier disclosed his 2023 earnings publicly on Logan Paul's Impaulsive podcast, telling Logan he made $38 million that year through YouTube ad revenue, brand deals, and investments. That ranked him the third-highest-earning YouTube creator in Forbes' 2023 reporting. His 2024 earnings are estimated at around $32 million, with his total net worth at approximately $45 million per Celebrity Net Worth.

Notably, Markiplier donates 100% of merchandise revenue to charity, a commitment he reaffirmed on Impaulsive. The bulk of his personal income comes from YouTube ad revenue, his Cloak fashion brand co-founded with Jacksepticeye, brand sponsorships, and his expanding filmmaking career anchored by the 2024 horror film Iron Lung.

Where the money comes from

Markiplier described his income breakdown publicly on Impaulsive: most earnings come from brand deals, ad revenue, and his investments. Notably, all merchandise revenue is donated to charity.

YouTube Ad Revenue
Main and side channels generate the bulk of his content income. Per Social Blade estimates, monthly averages between $2.6 million and $3.2 million in ad revenue.
Cloak
Gamer-focused fashion brand co-founded with Jacksepticeye. Reported a 3,900% revenue increase from 2019 to 2020. Now a major income contributor.
Brand Sponsorships
Long-running partnerships with Polaris, Endeavor, Robot Entertainment, and others. Specific deal values not publicly disclosed.
Iron Lung (2024 film)
Markiplier's feature directorial debut, based on the indie horror video game. Produced and starred in by him. Theatrical and streaming release details not publicly disclosed.
Distractible Podcast
Spotify-exclusive video podcast with Bob Muyskens and Wade Barnes. Multi-year content deal with Spotify.
Merchandise
Significant revenue from merchandise sales, but Markiplier publicly committed to donating 100% of merch profits to charity.

The Impaulsive disclosure

During his guest appearance on Logan Paul's Impaulsive podcast, Markiplier became one of the few major creators to openly discuss his income. He confirmed making $38 million in 2023, calling the figure "ungodly" and describing how his earnings come from a diversified mix of ad revenue, brand deals, and investments. The disclosure ranked him as the third-highest-earning YouTube creator on Forbes' 2023 list, behind only MrBeast and Logan Paul himself.

Markiplier also used the appearance to reiterate his standing commitment to donating merchandise revenue to charity, a practice he has maintained for several years.
