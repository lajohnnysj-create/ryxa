The short answer

KSI was ranked #2 on Forbes' 2023 Top Creators list with $24 million in earnings, behind only MrBeast. Celebrity Net Worth places his total net worth at $100 million as of 2024. However, his ownership stake in Prime Hydration could push his actual net worth significantly higher, with some estimates suggesting his Prime equity alone could be worth between $300 million and $600 million.

KSI's income is more diversified than almost any other creator. He earns from three personal YouTube channels (KSI, JJ Olatunji, KSIClips), four Sidemen group channels, his Prime co-ownership with Logan Paul, professional boxing purses, chart-topping music, and several Sidemen business ventures including Sidemen Clothing, Sides restaurants, the Side+ subscription app, XIX Vodka, and Best Cereal.

Where the money comes from

KSI's income spans entertainment, beverages, food, boxing, and music. His business empire is one of the most diversified of any creator.

Prime Hydration
Co-founded with Logan Paul in January 2022. Reached over $1.2 billion in annual sales by 2023. KSI's personal stake is the largest single contributor to his net worth.
YouTube
Three personal channels (KSI, JJ Olatunji, KSIClips) plus four Sidemen channels (Sidemen, MoreSidemen, SidemenReacts, SidemenShorts). Combined billions of views. $250K per video per Sports Illustrated estimates.
Boxing
Professional boxer with high-profile fights against Logan Paul (twice) and Tommy Fury. Multi-million dollar fight purses for major bouts.
Sidemen Ventures
Co-founder of Sidemen Clothing (2014), Sides restaurant chain, Side+ subscription app (2021), XIX Vodka (2022), and Best Cereal (2024). Revenue is shared among the group's seven members.
Music
Chart-topping rapper with 25+ singles, two studio albums, and four EPs. Multiple ticketed tours including Jump Around (2016), New Age (2019), and All Over The Place (2021-22).
Lunchly
Snack brand co-founded with Logan Paul and MrBeast in 2024. Lunchables-style boxed meals. Launched with significant attention despite public criticism.

The Prime equity question

The single largest variable in estimating KSI's true net worth is the valuation of his Prime Hydration stake. Prime reached over $1.2 billion in sales by 2023, and beverage industry analysts have valued similar high-growth brands at multiples of revenue. Celebrity Net Worth suggests KSI's company stake could add anywhere between $300 million and $600 million to his total net worth, though this is a private company so the actual value is not publicly disclosed.

KSI's earnings rose dramatically after Forbes ranked him #2 globally in 2023. The Times of London labeled him Britain's most influential digital figure in 2020. He has also appeared on Forbes' 30 Under 30 Europe in Entertainment (2021).
