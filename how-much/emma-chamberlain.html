The short answer

Celebrity Net Worth reports Emma Chamberlain earned $20 million from all endeavors between September 2022 and September 2023. Her total net worth is estimated at $30 million as of 2024, though some outlets place the figure as high as $70 million when factoring her ownership stake in Chamberlain Coffee.

Emma was named to Forbes' 30 Under 30 list. Chamberlain Coffee, where she serves as co-CEO with roughly 30 to 50 percent ownership, was valued at $54 million in 2022 and continues to expand with physical café locations and major retail distribution including Walmart, Target, Costco, and Sprouts.

Where the money comes from

Emma's income is anchored by Chamberlain Coffee equity, podcast and brand partnerships, and a relatively quiet but ongoing YouTube presence. Unlike YouTube-first creators who depend on upload frequency, her income now comes primarily from her business ownership and external deals.

Chamberlain Coffee
Founded 2019 when Emma was 18. Now co-CEO with estimated 30 to 50 percent equity ownership. Sold in 8,500+ retail stores including Walmart, Target, Costco, Sprouts. First physical café opened January 2025 at Westfield Century City Mall in Los Angeles.
Anything Goes Podcast
Weekly podcast launched in April 2019 (originally Stupid Genius). Became a Spotify exclusive in February 2023. Won Best Podcaster at the 12th Shorty Awards and Pop Podcast at the People's Choice Awards.
Louis Vuitton Partnership
Long-standing brand ambassador relationship with Louis Vuitton. Regular appearances at Vuitton fashion shows and high-profile campaigns.
Lancôme Partnership
Brand ambassador for Lancôme cosmetics. One of her major luxury beauty partnerships.
YouTube
12+ million subscribers on her main channel. Upload frequency has slowed substantially, but back catalog continues generating ad revenue.
Other Partnerships
Past and ongoing partnerships across luxury and lifestyle: Cartier, fashion week appearances. Featured in Time's 25 Most Influential People on the Internet in 2019.

The Chamberlain Coffee transition

Chamberlain Coffee is the central story of Emma's net worth. Founded as a mail-order coffee company in late 2019 when she was 18, it has grown into a major direct-to-consumer brand with retail distribution across 8,500+ US stores. Emma's promotion to co-CEO in 2024 signaled active operational involvement, not just figurehead status.

For Emma personally, Chamberlain Coffee represents significant equity that could materialize if the company exits via acquisition or IPO in future years. Her stake at 30 to 50 percent of a $54M valuation (per 2022) was already worth tens of millions; subsequent growth has likely increased that figure substantially.
