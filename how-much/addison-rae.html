The short answer

Influencer Marketing Hub estimates Addison Rae earns around $8.5 million per year, combining sponsored posts, her Item Beauty brand, acting roles, and music. Forbes ranked her #30 on its 2025 Top Creators list, attributing roughly $5 million in earnings that year. Celebrity Net Worth pegs her total net worth at $25 million as of 2025, with most outlets placing her in the $20 million to $25 million range.

Addison rose fast. In 2020, Forbes named her the highest-paid TikTok personality in the world at $5 million in pre-tax earnings, ahead of Charli D'Amelio that year. Per Celebrity Net Worth she grossed $9 million in 2021. Her income has since shifted away from being purely TikTok-driven, with a debut studio album, a headlining tour, film roles, and a beauty company now carrying a meaningful share of her earnings.

Where the money comes from

Addison's earnings started almost entirely as TikTok brand deals and have widened into beauty, music, film, and product ventures. The mix below is why outlets place her annual figure well above her TikTok income alone.

Sponsored Posts
Reported at $50,000 to $100,000 per brand post, with Hopper HQ estimating roughly $88,000 per sponsored TikTok. Her first paid deal was with Fashion Nova; later partners include Reebok, Hollister, Daniel Wellington, and L'Oreal.
American Eagle
Became a primary spokesperson for American Eagle, appearing across the brand's digital, print, and TV campaigns. One of her largest and most visible endorsement relationships.
Item Beauty
Co-founded the clean, minimalist cosmetics line Item Beauty under the Madeby Collective. Gives her direct product equity rather than a flat endorsement fee.
Music
Her debut studio album "Addison" (June 2025) debuted at #4 on the Billboard 200 with roughly 48,500 first-week units. Lead single "Diet Pepsi" has drawn close to 400 million Spotify streams. She followed with The Addison Tour across Europe, North America, and Australia.
Acting
Starred in Netflix's "He's All That" and the slasher film "Thanksgiving," with an upcoming role in Legendary's "Animal Friends" alongside Ryan Reynolds, Jason Momoa, and Aubrey Plaza.
Fragrance and Podcast
Launched the Addison Rae Fragrance and co-hosted the "Mama Knows Best" podcast with her mother, adding product and media income to the stack.

The TikTok-first business model

Addison started posting dance videos in 2019 and passed a million followers in under a year. She moved to Los Angeles, joined the Hype House collective, and by 2020 was the platform's highest-earning star, almost entirely on the strength of brand collaborations.

What followed is the now-familiar creator playbook: convert a single-platform audience into businesses that do not depend on that platform. Music, film, beauty, and fragrance now sit alongside her social income, which is why her annual earnings estimates land around $8.5 million even though her direct TikTok pay is a fraction of that.
