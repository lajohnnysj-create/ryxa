The short answer

Forbes ranked Khaby Lame #10 on its 2024 Top Creators list with estimated annual earnings of $20 million, making him the highest-earning Black creator on the list. His net worth was previously reported at $16.5 million per Forbes (2023) and has grown rapidly alongside his commercial deals.

In January 2026, Khaby signed a $975 million deal with Hong Kong-based Rich Sparkle Holding for exclusive 36-month global brand rights and the development of an AI version of his likeness. The company projects the partnership could generate over $4 billion in annual sales across beauty, fragrance, and apparel. The deal represents one of the largest commercial agreements involving any digital creator to date.

Where the money comes from

Khaby's income is unusually concentrated in brand sponsorships compared to other top creators. With minimal merchandise operations and no direct YouTube monetization at the same scale as YouTube-first creators, his per-post brand rates do most of the work.

Khaby has publicly stated his goal of breaking into Hollywood acting, with an interest in co-starring with Will Smith. Has appeared at the Venice Film Festival and judged the Cannes TikTok Short Film challenge.

The Rich Sparkle deal explained

In January 2026, Khaby's company Step Distinctive sold a stake to Nasdaq-listed Rich Sparkle Holding Ltd for approximately $975 million. The deal grants Rich Sparkle exclusive global rights to Khaby's brand for 36 months across sectors including beauty, fragrance, and apparel.

The agreement also authorizes Rich Sparkle to develop an "AI Digital Twin" of Khaby, using his face, voice, and behavioral models to create multilingual livestream content for global audiences. Per the press release, the partnership could generate over $4 billion in annual sales. Khaby retains a controlling share in Step Distinctive and continues to lead the company.
