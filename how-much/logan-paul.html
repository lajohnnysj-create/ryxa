The short answer

Forbes estimated Logan Paul's personal earnings at $9.8 million in 2024, ranking him #12 on the Top Creators list. Celebrity Net Worth places his annual income at at least $20 million when including business equity, performance fees, and brand deals. His total net worth is estimated at around $150 million, driven largely by his ownership stake in Prime Hydration, which generated over $1.2 billion in sales in 2023.

Logan's income mix is unusual for a creator. Direct social media revenue is now a minority of what he earns. The bulk comes from his Prime Hydration co-ownership with KSI, his WWE wrestling contract, and boxing match purses including high-profile fights against Floyd Mayweather and at WrestleMania.

Where the money comes from

Logan's income is unusually diversified for a creator. He owns equity in major consumer brands and earns wrestling and boxing income separately from his content business.

Prime Hydration
Co-founded with KSI in January 2022. Reportedly hit $250 million in first-year sales per Fortune. Surpassed $1.2 billion in 2023 per Financial Post. Logan's personal cut from equity is not publicly disclosed.
WWE Wrestling
Signed to WWE since June 2022, performing on a part-time basis. The Mirror reports his base salary at approximately $2 million per year, plus performance fees and merchandise.
Boxing
Earned an estimated $30 million from his loss to Tommy Fury per his own statement. The 2021 Floyd Mayweather exhibition match was speculated at $20 million for Logan.
Impaulsive Podcast
Launched November 2018. Long-running interview podcast with notable guests including Donald Trump. Generates significant ad revenue plus YouTube views.
YouTube
23+ million subscribers on his main channel. Once his primary income source, now a smaller share of total earnings.
Lunchly
Snack brand co-founded with KSI and MrBeast in 2024. Lunchables-style boxed meals. Faced public criticism but generated significant launch attention.

Net worth: roughly $150M

Celebrity Net Worth places Logan's net worth at around $150 million, driven heavily by his Prime Hydration equity stake. Forbes has not published an official net worth estimation for him, focusing instead on annual earnings. The headline gap between Forbes' annual figure ($9.8M) and Celebrity Net Worth's estimate ($20M+) likely reflects whether business equity is counted: Forbes typically tracks personal annual take, while Celebrity Net Worth factors in equity value.

Some estimates have placed him even higher, with one outlet suggesting his net worth could be approaching $245 million when factoring full Prime Hydration valuation. Those figures are speculative and not endorsed by Forbes or other primary sources.
