The short answer

Dude Perfect's annual income is estimated at approximately $50 million per Tasty Edits, with YouTube alone bringing in $20 to $25 million per year per Celebrity Net Worth. The group's net worth estimates vary widely depending on how their brand and business assets are valued, with Celebrity Net Worth placing it at $100 million and Tasty Edits suggesting a range of $50 to $125 million.

In 2024, Dude Perfect accepted a $100 million private equity investment from Highmount Capital, a media and technology investment firm. The deal positioned the group for further expansion including the planned Dude Perfect World, a $100 million theme park in Texas. The group operates under CEO Andrew Yaffe with a structure more like a traditional media company than a typical creator collective.

Where the money comes from

Dude Perfect operates as a sports entertainment brand rather than a typical creator channel. Their income spans every traditional media revenue stream plus an expanding investment portfolio.

YouTube Ad Revenue
$20-25 million annually across the main Dude Perfect channel plus Dude Perfect Plus, Dude Perfect Gaming, and Dude Perfect en Español. Main channel videos average 2 million views per day.
Highmount Capital Investment
Accepted $100 million PE investment in 2024 from Highmount Capital, a media and tech investment firm. Specific deal terms not publicly disclosed.
Brand Partnerships
Major partnerships with Columbia Sportswear, Burnley Football Club (official 2023/2024 junior retail shirt partner, plus equity investment), Nerf, and others. Often integrated into video content.
Dude Perfect World
Announced in 2023, a planned $100 million 30-acre theme park in Texas. Expected to add significant long-term revenue.
Merchandise and Apparel
Branded apparel and accessories sold via Dude Perfect's official store. Significant revenue stream supported by their loyal family-friendly audience.
Live Tours
Multi-city live tours featuring trick shots and stunts. Tickets typically sell out in major markets.

The five members

Dude Perfect formed in 2009 when five Texas A&M University roommates uploaded their first trick shot video. The members are Tyler "The Beard" Toney, twin brothers Cory and Coby Cotton, Garrett "The Purple Hoser" Hilbert, and Cody "The Tall Guy" Jones. According to Tasty Edits, Tyler Toney is the wealthiest member with a personal net worth between $10 million and $20 million, while Cody Jones is estimated at around $6 million. Specific income shares among the five members are not publicly disclosed.

The group operates DPHQ3, a new $5 million headquarters in Frisco, Texas featuring filming spaces, a putting course, a pickleball court, and a production studio.
