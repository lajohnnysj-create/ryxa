The short answer

Forbes ranked Charli D'Amelio #6 on its 2025 Top Creators list with estimated annual earnings of $23.5 million. Celebrity Net Worth pegs her total net worth at $30 million as of 2024, with other outlets estimating ranges from $25 million to $45 million depending on how her business equity is calculated.

Charli reportedly earned $17.5 million in 2021 alone, making her Forbes' highest-paid TikToker that year. Her income mix is now diversified across brand deals, her Hulu reality series, podcast and music projects, and her own product lines. Per The Independent, top creators like Charli typically earn 30 to 50 percent of their income from TikTok itself.

Where the money comes from

Charli's income sources are unusually diversified for a creator who broke through on a single platform. Brand partnerships, her family's reality TV career, and her own product ventures all contribute meaningfully.

TikTok Sponsorships
Minimum $100,000 per sponsored TikTok post per Celebrity Net Worth. Brand partners have included Sony, Chipotle, Revlon, Hollister, Morphe Cosmetics, Yoplait, and Dunkin'.
The Dunkin' Partnership
Long-running collaboration with Dunkin' Donuts including "The Charli" signature drink launched in 2020. Sparked a 20% sales boost for cold brew coffees and a 57% increase in app downloads the day of launch.
The D'Amelio Show (Hulu)
Family reality series ran for three seasons before being canceled. Earnings from the show were a significant contributor to her income during its run.
Be Happy Snacks
Snack company co-founded with sister Dixie D'Amelio. Adds direct product ownership to her revenue stack.
Acting and Voice Work
Film debut with a voice role in "StarDog and TurboCat" (2020). Continues to take on acting roles.
Other Products
Two books, podcast, nail polish line, mattress, makeup line, and a multi-product company. Per Celebrity Net Worth, her projects over recent years have generated over $70 million in combined revenue.

The TikTok-first business model

Charli was the first person to reach both 50 million and 100 million followers on TikTok. She holds a unique position as the platform's defining mainstream star and the highest-earning female TikTok personality on Forbes' 2019 list. Her rise from 2019 dance videos to global brand status happened faster than virtually any creator in the platform's history.

According to The Independent, top creators like Charli typically generate 30 to 50 percent of their income from TikTok itself, with the rest coming from external sponsorships, products, and media. That breakdown puts her TikTok-direct earnings in the $7-11.75 million range per year, with the remainder from her broader business empire.
