FAQ

Common questions about 1:1 Booking.

Do I need a Calendly account?
+
No. You can choose manual booking and handle scheduling yourself via email. If you want automated scheduling, you can use Calendly (free tier available) or Cal.com (free and open source).
Does Ryxa take a percentage of my booking revenue?
+
No. Ryxa takes 0%. Payments go directly to your connected Stripe account. The only fees are Stripe's standard processing fees.
Can I offer free sessions?
+
Yes. Set the price to $0 and clients can book for free. You'll still get an email notification with their contact info.
How do clients book after paying?
+
If you've connected Calendly or Cal.com, clients see an embedded scheduler right on the confirmation page after payment. For manual bookings, you'll receive an email with their info and reach out to schedule.
Can I create multiple services?
+
Yes. Create as many services as you want — each gets its own landing page, price, and booking method.
