Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ryxa. Can't find your answer? Email us at hello@ryxa.io

General
What is Ryxa? ▼

Ryxa is a suite of creator tools designed to help you grow smarter, protect your energy, and run your creator business. Our tools include a Follower Audit, Image Studio, QR Code Generator, and Invoice Generator, all in one dashboard.

Everything is built with privacy in mind. Most tools run entirely in your browser for speed and simplicity.

Do I need an account to use Ryxa? ▼

Yes, a free account is required to access the tools. Creating an account is free and takes less than a minute. No credit card required.

Signing in allows us to save your preferences, track your usage limits, and unlock features like audit history and saved settings.

What is the difference between Free, Pro, and Creator Max? ▼

Free gives you limited access to all tools — great for trying everything out.

Pro ($10/month) unlocks full access to most tools with higher limits and premium features. For example, the Follower Audit shows your full list with audit history, analytics, whitelist, snake list, and unfollower log. Link in Bio and Media Kit unlock branding removal, custom theme presets, and more.

Creator Max ($20/month) includes everything in Pro plus the Script Builder tool, fully custom themes (including custom backgrounds), expanded link counts, and other Max-only features.

See our pricing page for the full comparison.

Is Ryxa affiliated with Instagram or Meta? ▼

No. Ryxa is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Instagram or Meta in any way. We use data that Instagram allows you to export from your own account.

Why would this be important to me? ▼

If you are a creator, influencer, small business, or anyone who puts real time and energy into building an audience on Instagram, knowing who actually supports you back matters.

You follow people, engage with their content, support their businesses, cheer them on. But are they doing the same for you? Ryxa gives you clarity so you can make intentional decisions about who you give your energy to.

Beyond the follower audit, our other tools help you run your creator business, from generating professional invoices for brand deals to creating QR codes for your campaigns. Your follow is a form of support. You deserve to know if it is mutual. 🐍

Privacy & Safety
Is my data safe with Ryxa? ▼

Yes. We take privacy seriously and store only what's needed to operate the tools you use — your account info, subscription status, and the data you create using our tools (like your link in bio, media kit, scripts, tax entries, and other tool-specific content).

We never sell your data to third parties. For the full list of what we store and how, see our privacy policy.

Do you need my Instagram password? ▼

Absolutely not. We never ask for your Instagram password. The Follower Audit works using data files you export directly from Instagram yourself. No account access, no scraping, no risk to your account.

Will using Ryxa get my Instagram account banned? ▼

We do not connect to your Instagram account or use any automated access. The Follower Audit only processes data files that you export from Instagram yourself through their official Download Your Information feature, so our tool operates entirely outside of Instagram's platform.

Can I delete my account and data? ▼

Yes. You can request account and data deletion at any time by emailing us at hello@ryxa.io. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Billing
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time with no questions asked. When you cancel, you keep full Pro access until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan.

You can cancel directly from your dashboard at the bottom of the page.

Do you offer refunds? ▼

We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you are not satisfied. Email us at hello@ryxa.io and we will take care of it promptly. No hassle, no hard feelings.

Is my payment information secure? ▼

Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, one of the most trusted payment processors in the world. We never see or store your credit card details. Stripe handles all payment security and compliance.

What happens to my data if I cancel? ▼

Your data is preserved when you cancel. Your account downgrades to Free at the end of your billing period, but your audit history, snake list, whitelist, unfollower log, and all other Pro/Max data stays safely stored. If you resubscribe later — whether that's next month or next year — everything will be right where you left it. You can request permanent deletion at any time by emailing hello@ryxa.io.

How do I upgrade to Pro? ▼

You can upgrade from inside your dashboard. Click the "Upgrade to Pro" button at the top of the page or in any tool that has Pro-only features. You will be taken to a secure checkout page powered by Stripe.

Tools
How does the Follower Audit work? ▼

Instagram lets you download your own data as JSON files. You download those files from Instagram, upload them to Ryxa, and we instantly compare your followers list against your following list to show you who is not following you back.

Your Instagram export files are processed in your browser for the comparison, we do not store the raw file contents.

How long does Instagram take to send my data export? ▼

Instagram will email you a download link. It usually arrives within a few minutes, but can take up to 48 hours depending on your account size and Instagram's servers. This is outside our control.

Check our Instructions page for a step-by-step guide on how to request and download your data.

What image formats does the Image Studio support? ▼

Image Studio lets you crop, resize, and convert images. JPEG, PNG, and WebP work on all modern browsers. Additional formats (GIF, BMP, TIFF, AVIF, ICO) show up when your browser supports them.

Crop presets include Square (1:1), Portrait (4:5), Story/Reel (9:16), Landscape (16:9), and Wide (3:1) — sized for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and X. Everything happens in your browser; your images are not uploaded to our servers.

How many QR codes can I generate? ▼

Free users can generate 3 QR codes per day. Pro users get unlimited QR codes. Pro users also get custom color options for their QR codes and high-resolution downloads.

Is the Invoice Generator really free? ▼

Yes, Free users can create 3 invoices per day. Pro users get unlimited invoices plus the ability to upload their logo. All invoices can be downloaded as a PDF directly from your browser.

What if someone unfollowed me but also removed me as their follower? ▼

If someone unfollowed you AND removed you as their follower, they will not appear on your list at all. Ryxa works by comparing your followers list against your following list. If they are not in either list, there is nothing to compare.

But honestly? If they went through the trouble of removing themselves from your followers too, they did you a favor. They took their own trash out. 🗑️🐍
