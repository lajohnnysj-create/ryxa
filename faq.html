Frequently Asked Questions

General
What is FindTheSnakes? ▼

FindTheSnakes is a suite of creator tools designed to help you grow smarter, protect your energy, and run your creator business. Our tools include a Follower Audit, Image Converter, QR Code Generator, and Invoice Generator — all in one dashboard.

Everything is built with privacy in mind. Most tools run entirely in your browser for speed and simplicity.

Do I need an account to use FindTheSnakes? ▼

Yes, a free account is required to access the tools. Creating an account is free and takes less than a minute. No credit card required.

Signing in allows us to save your preferences, track your usage limits, and unlock features like audit history and saved settings.

What is the difference between Free and Pro? ▼

The Free plan gives you limited access to all tools — perfect for trying everything out. The Pro plan unlocks full access with higher limits and premium features.

For example, the Follower Audit on Free shows your first 3 results. Pro shows your full list, plus audit history, analytics, whitelist, snake list, and unfollower log. Image Converter goes from 3/day to 10/day with larger file sizes. Invoice Generator becomes unlimited with logo upload. See our pricing page for the full comparison.

Is FindTheSnakes affiliated with Instagram or Meta? ▼

No. FindTheSnakes is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Instagram or Meta in any way. We use data that Instagram allows you to export from your own account.

Why would this be important to me? ▼

If you are a creator, influencer, small business, or anyone who puts real time and energy into building an audience on Instagram, knowing who actually supports you back matters.

You follow people, engage with their content, support their businesses, cheer them on. But are they doing the same for you? FindTheSnakes gives you clarity so you can make intentional decisions about who you give your energy to.

Beyond the follower audit, our other tools help you run your creator business — from generating professional invoices for brand deals to creating QR codes for your campaigns. Your follow is a form of support. You deserve to know if it is mutual. 🐍

Privacy & Safety
Is my data safe with FindTheSnakes? ▼

Yes. We take privacy seriously. We only store what is necessary to provide the service — your account info, subscription status, and Pro feature data like your whitelist and audit history.

We store only what is necessary — your account email, subscription status, and feature data like your whitelist and audit history. We never sell your data to third parties.

Do you need my Instagram password? ▼

Absolutely not. We never ask for your Instagram password. The Follower Audit works using data files you export directly from Instagram yourself. No account access, no scraping, no risk to your account.

Will using FindTheSnakes get my Instagram account banned? ▼

No. Because we never connect to your Instagram account or use any automated access, there is zero risk of getting banned. We only use data that Instagram itself provides through their official data export feature.

Can I delete my account and data? ▼

Billing
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time with no questions asked. When you cancel, you keep full Pro access until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan.

You can cancel directly from your dashboard at the bottom of the page.

Do you offer refunds? ▼

We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you are not satisfied. Email us at [email protected] and we will take care of it promptly. No hassle, no hard feelings.

Is my payment information secure? ▼

Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, one of the most trusted payment processors in the world. We never see or store your credit card details. Stripe handles all payment security and compliance.

What happens to my data if I cancel? ▼

Your data is kept for 30 days after cancellation in case you change your mind and resubscribe. After 30 days, Pro-specific data like your audit history, snake list, whitelist, and unfollower log will be cleared. Your account itself remains active on the Free plan.

How do I upgrade to Pro? ▼

You can upgrade from inside your dashboard. Click the "Upgrade to Pro" button at the top of the page or in any tool that has Pro-only features. You will be taken to a secure checkout page powered by Stripe.

Tools
How does the Follower Audit work? ▼

Instagram lets you download your own data as JSON files. You download those files from Instagram, upload them to FindTheSnakes, and we instantly compare your followers list against your following list to show you who is not following you back.

Your Instagram export files are processed in your browser for the comparison — we do not store the raw file contents.

How long does Instagram take to send my data export? ▼

Instagram will email you a download link. It usually arrives within a few minutes, but can take up to 48 hours depending on your account size and Instagram's servers. This is outside our control.

Check our Instructions page for a step-by-step guide on how to request and download your data.

What image formats does the Image Converter support? ▼

The Image Converter supports converting between JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, BMP, TIFF, AVIF, and ICO formats. You can convert from any of these to any other. Conversion happens in your browser — your images are not stored on our servers.

Note: AVIF output requires Chrome or Firefox. Safari has limited support for some formats.

How many QR codes can I generate? ▼

Free users can generate 3 QR codes per day. Pro users get unlimited QR codes. Pro users also get custom color options for their QR codes and high-resolution downloads.

Is the Invoice Generator really free? ▼

Yes, Free users can create 3 invoices per day. Pro users get unlimited invoices plus the ability to upload their logo. All invoices can be downloaded as a PDF directly from your browser.

What if someone unfollowed me but also removed me as their follower? ▼

If someone unfollowed you AND removed you as their follower, they will not appear on your list at all. FindTheSnakes works by comparing your followers list against your following list. If they are not in either list, there is nothing to compare.

But honestly? If they went through the trouble of removing themselves from your followers too, they did you a favor. They took their own trash out. 🗑️🐍
