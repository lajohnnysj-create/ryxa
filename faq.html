Frequently Asked Questions

General
What is Ryxa? ▼

Ryxa is an all-in-one creator platform for building, sharing, and monetizing your work. Sell digital products, courses, and 1:1 bookings directly to your audience. Build a link in bio, media kit, and brand deal portal — all from one dashboard.

Everything is built with creators in mind: your tools, your audience, your income, in one place.

Do I need an account to use Ryxa? ▼

Yes, a free account is required. Creating one takes less than a minute and no credit card is required.

Your account is what saves your link in bio, media kit, products, courses, bookings, and everything else you build on Ryxa.

What is the difference between Free, Pro, and Creator Max? ▼

Free gives you essentials to build your presence — Link in Bio, Design Studio, Photo Editor, Grid Planner, Follow-Back Audit, QR Generator, Invoice Generator, Sign PDF, Subscribers, and Calendar.

Pro ($10/month) adds Media Kit, AI tools (background removal, rewrite, captions, thumbnail analyzer, contract analyzer), AI Script Builder, the Chatbox AI assistant, removes Ryxa branding, and unlimited usage on all included tools.

Creator Max ($20/month) unlocks the monetization suite — Course Builder, 1:1 Booking, Digital Products, Brand Deal CRM, Analytics, custom backgrounds, AI Clean Up, and expanded link counts.

See our pricing page for the full comparison.

Is Ryxa affiliated with Instagram or Meta? ▼

No. Ryxa is an independent platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Instagram or Meta in any way.

Privacy & Safety
Is my data safe with Ryxa? ▼

Yes. We take privacy seriously and store only what's needed to operate the tools you use — your account info, subscription status, and the data you create using our tools (like your link in bio, media kit, scripts, tax entries, and other tool-specific content).

We never sell your data to third parties. For the full list of what we store and how, see our privacy policy.

Do you need my Instagram password? ▼

Never. Ryxa never asks for your Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or any other social media password. Any future connections to your accounts will use the platform's official, secure API — never password-based access.

Will using Ryxa get my Instagram account banned? ▼

No. Ryxa is built to work safely with Instagram. We don't scrape data, automate actions, or do anything that violates Instagram's terms of service.

Any future Instagram connections will use Meta's official API with your explicit consent — the same way other approved apps connect to your account safely.

Can I delete my account and data? ▼

Yes. You can request account and data deletion at any time by emailing us at hello@ryxa.io. We will delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Billing
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your Pro or Creator Max subscription at any time with no questions asked. When you cancel, you keep full access until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan.

You can cancel directly from your dashboard at the bottom of the page.

Do you offer refunds? ▼

We offer refunds within 7 days of purchase if you are not satisfied. Email us at hello@ryxa.io and we will take care of it promptly. No hassle, no hard feelings.

Is my payment information secure? ▼

Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, one of the most trusted payment processors in the world. We never see or store your credit card details. Stripe handles all payment security and compliance.

What happens to my data if I cancel? ▼

Your data is preserved when you cancel. Your account downgrades to Free at the end of your billing period, but everything you've built — your link in bio, media kit, products, courses, bookings, brand deals, and audit history — stays safely stored.

If you resubscribe later, whether that's next month or next year, everything will be right where you left it. You can request permanent deletion at any time by emailing hello@ryxa.io.

How do I upgrade to Pro or Creator Max? ▼

You can upgrade from inside your dashboard. Click the upgrade button at the top of the page or in any tool that has Pro or Max-only features. You'll be taken to a secure checkout page powered by Stripe.

Selling & Payments
What can I sell on Ryxa? ▼

You can sell digital products like ebooks, presets, templates, and downloads. Build and sell online courses with modules and lessons. Offer 1:1 bookings for coaching, consulting, photography, tutoring, or any private session.

All from one dashboard, all on a single creator landing page.

How do I sell digital products? ▼

Upload your file, set a price, and share your product link. Buyers can purchase directly through Stripe and get instant access to their download in their Ryxa Hub.

You can sell ebooks, presets, templates, design files, and any other digital download. Each product gets its own shareable URL.

What fees does Ryxa take on sales? ▼

Ryxa takes 0% transaction fees on every sale. You keep what you make.

Stripe processes payments and charges its standard processing fee (around 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction). That fee goes to Stripe, not Ryxa.

Ryxa makes money through Pro and Creator Max subscriptions, not by taking a cut of your sales.

Can I connect my existing Stripe account? ▼

Yes. Ryxa works with any existing Stripe account. Connect it once and your sales flow directly to your Stripe balance.

If you don't have a Stripe account yet, you can create one in minutes when you set up selling on Ryxa.

What is Ryxa Hub? ▼

Ryxa Hub is where buyers access everything they've purchased from creators on Ryxa. Their courses, digital products, and 1:1 bookings live in one place, accessible from any device.

Buyers can also browse the Ryxa marketplace to discover new creators and offerings.
