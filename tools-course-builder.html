FAQ

Common questions about Course Builder.

Does Ryxa take a percentage of my sales?
+
No. Ryxa takes 0% transaction fees on your course revenue. Payments go directly to your Stripe account. The only fees are Stripe's standard processing fees (around 2.9% + 30 cents per transaction).
Can I create free courses?
+
Yes. Set the price to $0 and anyone can enroll without payment. Great for lead magnets or building your audience.
Where do buyers access the course after purchasing?
+
Buyers access courses through the Ryxa Hub at ryxa.io/learn. They sign in, see all their purchased courses, and can track their progress lesson by lesson.
Do I need a separate video host like YouTube or Vimeo?
+
No. Ryxa includes video hosting. Upload your video directly in the lesson editor and we handle the rest. Your students stream the video right inside the course player.
Can I attach files like PDFs or worksheets to lessons?
+
Yes. Each lesson can have up to 5 downloadable files. Supported types include PDFs, Office docs, images, audio, design files (PSD, AI, Sketch, Figma), fonts, and ZIPs. Each file is capped at 50 MB.
How do quizzes work?
+
You can add one quiz at the end of any module with up to 10 multiple-choice questions. Choose whether students must pass to unlock the next module, or whether the quiz is just for practice and they can move on regardless.
