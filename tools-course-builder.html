FAQ

Common questions about Course Builder.

Does Ryxa take a percentage of my sales?
+
No. Ryxa takes 0% of your course revenue. Payments go directly to your Stripe account. The only fees are Stripe's standard processing fees (2.9% + $0.30).
Can I create free courses?
+
Yes. Set the price to $0 and anyone can enroll without payment. Great for lead magnets or building your audience.
Where do buyers access the course after purchasing?
+
Buyers access courses through the Learning Hub at ryxa.io/learn. They sign in, see all their purchased courses, and can track their progress lesson by lesson.
Is there a limit on how many courses I can create?
+
No limit. Create as many courses as you want with unlimited modules and lessons.
