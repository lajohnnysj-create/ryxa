FAQ

Common questions about Grid Planner.

Does this post for me?
+
No. Grid Planner is a planning tool. You screenshot your plan or just remember the order, then post manually on Instagram in the right sequence. Auto-posting requires Instagram API access that we'd rather not have.
Can I import my existing Instagram grid?
+
Not directly. You'd need to download your photos from Instagram and upload them.
How many photos can I plan?
+
Up to 18 photos at a time. Perfect for planning your next 6 rows of posts. Once you're done, post the photos in order and start a fresh planning session.
Are my photos uploaded to your servers?
+
Only when you save (Pro feature). Free users plan in-session. Photos stay in your browser and are never uploaded. Pro users save their grid to access it from any device. Saved files are private and only visible to you. Delete anytime.
Can I use this for TikTok or other platforms?
+
Grid Planner is designed for the Instagram 3-column feed. For TikTok and YouTube, the order matters less since they're algorithm-driven.
