📋 Step-by-step guide

How to Download Your Instagram Data

Follow these steps to get your Instagram data export. Upload the ZIP file directly to Ryxa, no unzipping needed.

1
Open Instagram Settings
  • Open Instagram on your phone
  • Go to your profile tab
  • Tap the hamburger menu (☰) in the top right
  • Tap Accounts Center
2
Request Your Data Export
  • Tap Your information and permissions
  • Tap Export your information
  • Tap Create export
  • Select your Instagram account
  • Tap Export to device
3
Select Followers and Following Only
  • Tap Customize information
  • Clear all sections except Followers and following
  • Tap Save
4
Set Export Settings

Make sure both of these are set correctly before continuing:

✓
Date range Set to All time
✓
Format Set to JSON (not HTML)
5
Submit and Wait for the Email
  • Tap Create files or Start export
  • Instagram will email you a download link when ready
  • This usually takes a few minutes but can take up to 48 hours
6
Download the ZIP and Upload It
  • Open the email from Instagram and click the Download button
  • Save the ZIP file to your computer, do not extract it
  • Come back to your Ryxa dashboard
  • Open Follow-Back Audit and upload the ZIP directly
  • We will find the right files automatically and run your analysis
💡 The ZIP is named something like instagram-username-2026-04-01.zip. Upload it exactly as downloaded, no unzipping needed.
✓ Large accounts are fully supported

If you have a large follower list, Instagram splits your data across multiple files inside the ZIP. Ryxa finds and processes all of them automatically.
