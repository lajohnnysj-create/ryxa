📋 Step-by-step guide

How to Download Your
Instagram Data

Follow these steps to export your followers and following files so FindTheSnakes can analyze them.

💡 For the best experience, complete these steps using a web browser on your phone or computer — not the Instagram app.
1
Go to Settings
  • Open Instagram
  • Go to your profile
  • Tap More (☰) → Settings
  • Tap See more in Accounts Center
2
Export Your Information
  • Tap Your information and permissions
  • Tap Export your information
  • Tap Create export
  • Select your account
  • Choose Export to device
  • Tap Customize information
3
Select Only Followers and Following
  • Tap Clear all for each category to deselect everything
  • Select only Followers and following
4
Set Your Export Settings

Make sure you select the following settings before continuing:

✓
Date range Set to All time
✓
Format Set to JSON
5
Submit Your Request
  • Tap Start export
  • Instagram will email you when your download is ready — this can take a few minutes to a few hours
6
Download and Open Your Files
  • Open the email from Instagram and click the download link
  • Download the ZIP file
  • Extract the ZIP file (right-click → Extract All on Windows)
  • Locate your JSON files inside the extracted folder
⚠️
You May Have Multiple Followers Files

If you have a large follower list, Instagram automatically splits your followers across multiple files. This is completely normal — just upload all of them.

followers_1.json
followers_2.json
followers_3.json

FindTheSnakes supports uploading multiple files at once — just select them all when uploading.
