FAQ

Common questions about Digital Products.

What kinds of files can I sell?
+
PDFs, ebooks, design files (PSD, AI, Figma), templates (Notion, Canva), presets (Lightroom, Capture One), LUTs, audio (MP3, WAV, FLAC), video (MP4, MOV, WEBM), fonts (OTF, TTF), Procreate brushes, 3D models, archives (ZIP, RAR), and most common image formats. Files are checked for safety on upload. Executable file types and scripts are not allowed. If you want a streaming video player with progress tracking for a paid course, use Course Builder instead.
What are the file size limits?
+
Each individual file can be up to 1 GB. A single product can include multiple files. Across your whole account, you can store up to 10 GB of downloadable files (shared with course downloads). If you need to sell larger files, reach out and we'll work with you.
Does Ryxa take a percentage of my sales?
+
No. Ryxa takes 0%. Payments go directly to your connected Stripe account. The only fees are Stripe's standard processing fees.
Can I offer free products?
+
Yes. Set the price to $0 and you have an instant lead magnet. Buyers still create a free account to access the download, so you can build your audience.
How do buyers download what they bought?
+
After payment, buyers are sent to their Ryxa Hub where their purchases live. They can download the files anytime, with no expiration. They also get a confirmation email with a direct link to the Hub.
Can I sell multiple files in one product?
+
Yes. Bundle as many files as you want into a single product, up to your 10 GB account storage. Useful for preset packs, multi-format templates, brand kits, and similar products.
Can buyers share the download link?
+
Download links are time-limited signed URLs generated for each buyer's session. They expire after a few minutes. Buyers can always re-download from their Ryxa Hub, but they can't share a permanent public link to your file.
