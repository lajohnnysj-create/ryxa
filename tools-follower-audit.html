FAQ

Common questions about Follow-Back Audit.

Why don't you just connect to Instagram directly?
+
Because Instagram bans accounts that use third-party follower-checking apps. Other tools risk your account. By having you upload your own data export, we keep your account safe and avoid Meta's ToS issues entirely.
How do I get my Instagram data export?
+
Open Instagram → Settings → Accounts Center → Your Information & Permissions → Download your information. Choose 'Followers and following' and submit. Instagram emails you a download link within 24 hours (usually faster).
Is my follower data stored on your servers?
+
Your raw Instagram export is not. Ryxa reads the ZIP file directly in your browser to run the audit, the file itself is never uploaded. The lists you choose to save, like your Whitelist and your Snake List, are stored securely in your Ryxa account so they're there when you come back.
Will running this get my account banned?
+
No. You're just downloading your own data and analyzing it. Instagram allows this. It's a privacy right. We never access your account or interact with Instagram in any way.
Does this work for TikTok or Twitter?
+
Currently Instagram-only.
