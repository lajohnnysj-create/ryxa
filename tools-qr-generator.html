FAQ

Common questions about QR Generator.

Is my QR data sent to any tracking service?
+
No. The QR code is generated in your browser. We never see your URL or any data about who scans it. The QR is yours, free and clear.
Can the QR redirect through your domain?
+
We don't redirect. Your QR encodes the URL directly, which means: faster scans, no dependency on us, and your QRs keep working forever even if Ryxa goes away.
What's the maximum URL length?
+
Technically QR codes support up to ~2,953 characters, but for reliable scanning we recommend under 200. For long URLs, use a URL shortener first.
Do I need to sign up?
+
Yes. The QR Generator is part of the Ryxa dashboard, so you'll need a free account to access it.
Will the QR break if I lose the original file?
+
No. Once a QR is generated and downloaded, it's just an image. Save it anywhere: your computer, Dropbox, Google Drive. The QR encodes the URL inside the image itself, not a reference to our servers.
