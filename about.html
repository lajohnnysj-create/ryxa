About

Built for creators,
by a creator

Ryxa exists because I couldn't find what I needed. So I built it.

Johnny La
Founder, Ryxa
@thejohnnyla

Where it started

When I started taking content creation seriously, I realized pretty quickly that looking professional costs money. Not just equipment or editing software, but all the little tools you need to actually run things. A page for your links. Something to build a media kit. A way to send invoices. A place to manage brand deals. Each one was a separate subscription, and each one added up.

I remember looking at my monthly expenses and thinking, there has to be a better way. I just needed simple tools that worked. I didn't need enterprise features or flashy dashboards built for agencies. I needed something made for creators like me.

I couldn't find it

I spent a lot of time looking for something affordable that brought everything together. One place where I could manage my links, pitch brands, track deals, and handle the business side of creating without paying a fortune.

Nothing fit. Everything was either too expensive, too complicated, or only solved one piece of the puzzle. I kept thinking someone should just build this. Eventually I realized that someone was going to have to be me.

So I built it

I started building Ryxa one tool at a time. Each one came from a real need I had as a creator. I wasn't trying to build the next big platform. I just wanted tools that were simple, worked well, and didn't cost a fortune to use.

That mindset is still what drives every decision. If a feature doesn't help a real creator solve a real problem, it doesn't make it in. No bloat, no fluff. Just useful things that actually matter when you're trying to grow.

What it is now

Today Ryxa is a full suite of creator tools in one workspace. Free to start, and if you need more, Pro is $10 a month. That's less than what most people pay for just one tool somewhere else. For creators who are working with brands and need everything, Creator Max is $20 a month.

This isn't built by some big company. It's built by one person who actually uses it, who knows what it feels like to be a creator trying to make things work without burning through money on tools.

Keep going

If you're here, you're already doing something most people won't. You're putting yourself out there, building something, creating something real. That takes guts. The tools you use along the way should support that, not slow you down or empty your pockets.

Ryxa is here to be on your side. And we're just getting started.
