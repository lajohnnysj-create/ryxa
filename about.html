Built for creators,

FindTheSnakes started as one tool and grew into something bigger — a suite of practical tools that help creators work smarter and grow intentionally.

Johnny La
Founder, FindTheSnakes

How it started

I was frustrated. As someone building an audience on Instagram, I kept wondering — who was actually following me back? Who was I supporting that wasn't returning the favor? I looked for tools to answer that question and found nothing that felt trustworthy. Most required your Instagram password. Others were sketchy apps fishing for data. Some cost way too much for something so simple.

So I built my own. The Follower Audit was the first tool — private, browser-based, no password required. Just upload your data export and see the truth. It struck a chord with creators who were tired of feeling in the dark about their own audience.

Why it grew into more

After talking to creators who were using the Follower Audit, I kept hearing the same thing — they needed other tools too. Tools for their business. Tools that should be simple but always seemed to cost too much or require signing up for something massive.

So I kept building. An Image Converter that works entirely in your browser. A QR Code Generator for campaigns and bios. An Invoice Generator for brand deals. All in one dashboard. All affordable. All built with the same philosophy — simple, private, and actually useful.

The philosophy

Creator tools should not cost a fortune. You should not need a separate app for every little task. And your tools should just work — without friction, without complexity.

FindTheSnakes is built on those three ideas. Free to start. Pro at a price that makes sense — $10 a month, less than a lunch. Simple by design, always.

This is a tool built by someone who actually uses it, for people who actually need it. That will never change.

What we're building next

More tools are coming. The focus will always be the same — practical, affordable, and built around what creators actually need. If you have a tool you wish existed, send us a message. We are listening.
