Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your Ryxa account and what happens to your data.

Ryxa™ is a product of MRLA Media LLC. You can delete your Ryxa account and its associated data at any time, directly from within the app. This page explains how to do it, and exactly what is removed and what is kept.

Delete your account from the app

If you can sign in, this is the fastest way:

  1. Open Ryxa and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm when prompted. Your account and data are permanently deleted right away.

Can't sign in?

If you're unable to access your account, email us at hello@ryxa.io from the email address associated with your account and request deletion. We'll verify your identity and process the deletion for you.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your data from Ryxa. This includes:

  • Your profile, username, and login credentials
  • Your Link in Bio, Media Kit, and any published pages
  • Courses, digital products, and 1:1 booking setups you created
  • Brand Deal CRM data: deals, brand contacts, contracts, invoices, and messages
  • Invoices, saved logos, tax entries, scripts, grid plans, and audit history
  • Uploaded files and images stored with your account
  • Subscriber lists and analytics tied to your account

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, your public pages stop resolving and your data cannot be recovered.

What is kept, and for how long

Payment records are held by Stripe, not Ryxa. Payments you made or received through Ryxa are processed by Stripe. Stripe retains transaction records under its own policies and applicable financial and tax laws, independently of your Ryxa account. Deleting your Ryxa account does not delete records held by Stripe.

Ryxa may retain a minimal, non-identifying internal marker to confirm an account was deleted, and may keep limited records where required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements. These records are kept only as long as necessary for those purposes and are not used to re-identify you.

Deleting individual data without closing your account

You don't have to delete your whole account to remove specific data. While signed in, you can delete individual items directly in the app at any time, and they're removed right away. For example:

  • Remove individual links, blocks, or your entire Link in Bio
  • Delete invoices, courses, digital products, or 1:1 booking setups
  • Delete brand deals, contracts, and uploaded files
  • Remove your avatar, logo, or other uploaded images
  • Clear scripts, tax entries, grid plans, and audit history

If you'd like us to delete specific data on your behalf, email hello@ryxa.io from your account's email address and let us know what you'd like removed.

Questions about deletion or your data? Contact us at hello@ryxa.io. For more on how we handle your information, see our Privacy Policy.
