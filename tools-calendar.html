FAQ

Questions about Calendar

What shows up on my calendar automatically?
+
Sessions booked through your 1:1 booking page and campaign dates from your Brand Deal CRM appear on their own, each labeled by type. You can also add your own events anytime.
Does it sync with Google Calendar?
+
Yes. Connect your Google account and Ryxa keeps a calendar in Google up to date with your events, so they show alongside the rest of your schedule there. You can disconnect at any time.
If I add an event, does it affect my booking availability?
+
It does. Adding an event blocks that time in your 1:1 booking availability, so bookers see fewer open slots. They never see what you have scheduled, only that the time is taken.
Can I edit synced bookings and brand deals?
+
Booking sessions stay tied to the booking they came from, and brand deal dates are managed in the Brand Deal CRM, so those are kept in sync for you. Events you create yourself are fully editable.
Is Calendar free?
+
Yes. Calendar is included free for everyone on Ryxa, including booking and brand deal sync, your own events, and the Google Calendar connection.
