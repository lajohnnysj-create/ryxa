Linktree is the most recognized link in bio tool in the world. When someone says "link in bio," most people picture Linktree. That kind of brand recognition is hard to compete with, and for good reason. It does what it does well.

What Linktree Does Well

Linktree is a great link page. Ryxa is a creator workspace that includes a great link page.

Creator tools: This is where the gap is widest. Ryxa includes a brand deal CRM, media kit, AI design studio, contract analyzer, script builder, thumbnail analyzer, and more. Linktree focuses primarily on the link page experience with commerce features built around it.

Who Should Use Linktree

If your strategy is purely about driving traffic to other platforms like YouTube, Spotify, or your own website, Linktree does that job simply and effectively.
