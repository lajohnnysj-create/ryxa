How Do I Change My Password

Ryxa changes your password through a secure reset link sent to your email, rather than a field where you type a new password directly. There are two ways to start that process: from the login screen if you are signed out, or from your dashboard settings if you are signed in. Both are below.

If you are signed out: use the login screen

Use this if you cannot get into your account, for example because you have forgotten your password.

  1. Go to Ryxa and open the sign in window.
  2. Type your account email address into the email field.
  3. Click the Forgot password? link.
  4. You will see the message "Password reset email sent! Check your inbox."
  5. Open the email from Ryxa and click the reset link inside it.
  6. The link opens a page where you can set your new password.

Enter your email before clicking the link. The Forgot password option sends the reset email to whatever address is in the email field, so that field needs to be filled in first.

If you are signed in: use your dashboard settings

Use this if you are already logged in and simply want to change your password.

  1. Open your Dashboard.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Find the password section and click Send password reset email.
  4. Ryxa sends a reset link to the email address on your account. You will see a confirmation that the email is on its way.
  5. Open the email and click the reset link to set your new password.

Because you are already signed in, this option always sends the link to your account's own email address. There is nothing to type.

Setting the new password

Both methods send you to the same place: a secure page, opened from the link in your email, where you enter your new password. Once you save it, your password is changed and you can sign in with it right away.

A few things worth knowing:

  • The reset link is time limited. If too much time passes, request a new one and use the most recent email.
  • If the email does not arrive within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder.
  • Always make sure the email is genuinely from Ryxa before clicking. We will only ever send reset links to the email address already on your account.

If you signed up with Google

If you created your Ryxa account using the Sign in with Google option, your access is managed through your Google account, not a Ryxa password. In that case there is no Ryxa password to change, and you would update your password with Google instead.

Still stuck?

If you are not receiving the reset email or are having trouble signing in, contact us and we will help you get back into your account.
